Palace of Oblivion

Video game
2020-2021

Memory is always looking for its inorganic supplements one after another in oblivion – graffiti, journal, RAM – memory needs to be externalized in order to persist. However, the Internet lacks a clear definition of data storage at its protocol layer (Stateless protocols), and therefore is in a state of amnesia. Under these circumstances, search engines, data centers, and decentralized storage protocols compete for the capability to construct collective memory.
Palace of Oblivion uses Simonides of Ceos’s method of “Memory Palace” as a reference. By tracing specific items, it reconstructs the space where the author lived in the 1990s through digital modeling. Meanwhile, using childhood memories as clues, the artist used crawler software to search for relevant pictures from the Internet, organized and uploaded them to the IPFS (InterPlanetary File System). These unchangeable image data correspond to their unique hash codes (CID). Therefore, they are kept on the Internet almost permanently, and visitors can find them by triggering some hidden clues. In the process of crawling the images, the artist found that these image data seem to have their own unique narratives in specific historical periods, such as the child labor in European factories that occurred frequently.
Individuals have the right to share the public memory; individual and public memory’s rights should not be seized or manufactured by big data algorithms and technological means available to few. How to handle memory, is a question for social technologies on how to ensure the rights of individual and public memories.



遗忘宫

游戏
2020-2021

记忆总是在遗忘中寻觅它一个又一个无机的补充物 -- 涂鸦、计事薄、内存，记忆需要体外化才能持存。互联网记忆是个体记忆的分布式涌现。然而，互联网在其协议层缺乏对数据储存的明确定义（无状态协议 stateless protocol），因而处于一种“失忆状态”。环绕这一状况，搜索引擎、数据中心、分布式储存协议展开角逐，以争夺构建集体记忆的能力。

＂遗忘宫＂以 Simonides of Ceos 的“记忆宫殿”方法为参照，通过对具体物品的追溯，用数码建模重新构建了作者 90 年代生活的空间。另一方面，以童年记忆为线索，作者用爬虫软件搜索了互联网上的相关图片，将其整理、上传到分布式存储与共享协议IPFS(InterPlanetary File System) 上，这些不可更改的图像数据对应着其独一无二的哈希码 (CID)。因而几乎被永久性地保存在互联网上，并被隐藏在房间中的某个角落，你可以通过触发隐藏的线索，来寻找它们。

个体拥有共享公共记忆的权力，个体和公共记忆权不应被大数据演算 和少数科技所攫取和制造。如何安顿记忆，是对如何保证个体和公共记 忆权的社会性技术的发问。
