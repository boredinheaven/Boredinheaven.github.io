​ Xiyue Hu was born in 1991 and currently lives in Bordeaux. She received her MFA degree at Academy of fine arts of Brera (Milan) in 2017.

Group exhibitions:
"Winter Salon Strasbourg", Salle des fêtes de la Bourse, Strasbourg (2018)
"Dreams", Espace 29, Bordeaux (April 2019).
"Night Mess", Espace Saint-Rémi, Bordeaux (May 2019).
"Stand by", 5UN7 Gallery, Bordeaux (July 2019).
"No idea-flow", x² Space, Shanghai (September 2019).

Xiyue Hu utilizes computer game scene, painting and installation, as well as combines text and sound to create a unique perception experience. Her practice involves the relationship between memory, technology and writing

胡汐悦将电脑游戏场景、绘画和装置作为创作媒介，并在游戏场景的制作中，综合文本和声音来制造独特的感官体验。她的创作在内容上涉及记忆，书写以及技术之间的关系。
