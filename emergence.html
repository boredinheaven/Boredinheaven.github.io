Emergence

2020-2021
Algorithm-generated sound animation
4'30''/1'56'' Loop


This project is a series of works with programming simulations and virtual space based on games. To some extent, they present a similar logic -- complex patterns emerge from simple deterministic rules. I used a cellular automaton to simulate two different types of cells that interacted with each other and evolved into a complex structure, all driven by the power of life and death. In such a dynamic system, some biological images are mapped into it, disturbing the whole process. This project is issued in NFT.



涌现

2020-2021
算法生成有声动画
4'30''/1'56''循环


《涌现》是一系列作品，以细胞自动机模拟黏菌的生长繁殖，通过模拟，几种不同性质的细胞之间彼此交互，受生与死的力量驱动而演化出了反应扩散系统。在初始情况下，我将一些生物、地理和历史图像作为养料，投入模拟器，复杂结构逐渐由简单的确定性规则中涌现出来。动画声音采样自AI生成音乐，通过混音完成制作。 该项目以NFT 铸币发行。
